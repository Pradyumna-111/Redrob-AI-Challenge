You are a Principal AI Engineer, Staff Backend Engineer, and Senior Product Architect.

Build a complete production-ready AI-powered Candidate Intelligence and Ranking Platform for the Redrob Data & AI Challenge.

The goal is to rank candidates the way an experienced recruiter would, not through keyword matching but through semantic understanding, contextual reasoning, career trajectory analysis, and explainable AI.

## Problem Statement

Recruiters often miss strong candidates because keyword-based systems fail to understand context.

Build an AI system that:

1. Understands job descriptions.
2. Understands resumes and candidate profiles.
3. Understands career progression.
4. Understands project relevance.
5. Understands domain expertise.
6. Understands behavioral signals.
7. Produces a ranked shortlist of candidates.
8. Explains every ranking decision.

The system must prioritize actual fit over keyword overlap.
